Questions before your first scan?

What does BCSentinel analyze?

BCSentinel scans your Business Central data quality across key areas such as customers, vendors, items, finance, purchasing, sales, and process-related records.

Which data is transferred?

BCSentinel transfers tenant and company identification, metadata, configuration data, scan results, findings, and aggregated quality metrics needed for the analysis.

Are API tokens used in reports or URLs?

No. API tokens should not appear in reports or URLs. Dashboard embed uses short-lived embed tokens, and report share links use expiring links.

What is a scan credit?

A scan credit allows one paid deep scan. Assessment and Validation Check each include one scan credit.

What is the difference between Assessment and Validation Check?

Assessment is the first deep scan. Validation Check is the follow-up scan after cleanup to confirm progress and refresh the report view.

When does monitoring make sense?

Monitoring makes sense when data quality should be checked continuously, for example after an initial cleanup or for business-critical processes.

How long do I have access to dashboard, issues, and reports?

Assessment and Validation Check provide seven days of access. Monitoring keeps access active while the subscription is active.

Does BCSentinel work with Business Central Cloud?

Yes. BCSentinel is prepared for Business Central Cloud scenarios and does not require direct database access.

Is BCSentinel already live in Microsoft AppSource?

No. AppSource readiness is in preparation; the current flow is for pilot and direct customer onboarding.
